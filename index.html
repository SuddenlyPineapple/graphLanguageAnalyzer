Instruction:

Enter some text below to make a graph of language. When you click on "Click to Analyze!" your text will be automatically cleared form any special chars. If you want analyze text with special chars or language like morse alphabet choose second option.

Legend:
- a start point, which connects all first letters of words,
- first letter of word,
- letter of the word on which word can't end,
- letter of the word where reading can end. When it's connected to start it means that your grammar has single chars also. In short it can start and end on that node in some cases.
